Lifting you off the street

The Problem

Newly homeless people don't have knowledge of available resources and struggle with the isolation of life on the streets.

Our Solution

To tackle this issue, we will install HelpingHands units, interactive displays with access to a helpline, across the city.

HelpingHands allows you to see available resources within a city.

HelpingHands also allows you to call a volunteer for assistance.

HelpingHands Prototype

Choose an option from the home screen

A map with relevant locations appears on the screen

After selecting a location, directions and specific information appear

If you select talk, the topics you can speak with a volunteer appear

Once you've selected an option, you are prompted to call a helpline

The blue end call button is a solution to the issue of red-green colorblindness
