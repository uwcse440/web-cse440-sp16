Overview

Problem

 COMMUNITY GARDENS IN NUMBERS

  • Over 6000 community gardens in the United States
  • 88 community gardens in Seattle alone

 ISSUES FACED BY GARDENS

  • Unequal participation and sharing by community members
  • In Seattle, 1-2 year wait time to get plot in community garden

 WHY THIS IS A PROBLEM

  • 14% of American households have limited access to fresh food
  • Fresh produce can be far away or too expensive

Our Solution

A tablet interface situated at the community garden sites to improve garden maintenance and the sense of community.

View the health of the garden as a whole

Learn details about the health of individual plots

Apply to volunteer on another individual's plot
